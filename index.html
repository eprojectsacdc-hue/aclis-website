About ACLIS

ACLIS is an independent engineering and advisory practice supporting energy infrastructure, renewable energy, battery storage and sustainable mobility projects.


We provide technical expertise, engineering support and strategic advisory services to developers, investors, EPC contractors, utilities and asset owners throughout the project lifecycle.

Services

Grid Connection Advisory

Connection strategy, technical reviews, grid compliance assessments and support throughout the DSO/TSO connection process.

Power Systems Engineering

Electrical studies, power system analysis, network assessments, reactive power compensation and engineering reviews.

Renewable Energy Solutions

Technical support for solar PV, wind and hybrid renewable energy projects.

BESS Solutions

Battery Energy Storage System assessment, technology evaluation, grid integration and project optimization.

Technical Due Diligence

Independent technical assessments for investors, lenders, developers and project acquisitions.

Electrical Design

Preparation and review of electrical designs, technical specifications, single-line diagrams and project documentation.

Agrivoltaic Development

Technical support for agrivoltaic projects, concept development and land-use optimization.

E-Mobility & EV Charging Infrastructure

Feasibility studies, electrical design, grid connection support and implementation advisory for EV charging infrastructure projects.
